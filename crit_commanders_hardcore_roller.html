Rules / Prizes 

Crit Commanders Hardcore Classic Challenge!
Hello all, and welcome to the Crit Commanders’ Hardcore Classic Challenge! The rules will be outlined here and may be updated as we encounter new/different issues during the event. The Event start date will be 11 July 2026 and will end 25 July 2026!

Prizes
1st Prize: 1 Class and Spec appropriate item to HR in ANY raid (or 5,000g Cash prize substitute)
2nd Prize: A Turbo Charged Flying Machine (or 2,000g Cash prize substitute)
3rd Prize: One (1) 22-Slot Bag (or 1,400g Cash Prize Substitute)

Rules
This will take place on a Blizzard Official Hardcore Server. So make sure you have the client downloaded.
Your allegiance to your faction, your race, your class, and your professions will be chosen at random during the dice roll event. Guidelines of this are listed in another section below.
We will be playing Solo and self-found which means no Auction House.
No AOE grinding. Regardless of class and spec.
People may team up to do dungeons ONLY.
If you die, you may make a new character of the same faction, race, class and specialization as was chosen for you by RNG AS MANY TIMES AS YOU WANT!

RNG Dice Roll Legend:
Faction – 1 for Alliance 2 for Horde
Race if Alliance – 1 for Night Elf, 2 for Human, 3 for Gnome, 4 for Dwarf
Race if Horde – 1 for Troll, 2 for Orc, 3 for Undead, 4 for Tauren
Gender – 1 for Man, 2 for Woman
Class – 1 for Hunter, 2 for Warrior, 3 for Rogue, 4 Warlock, 5 for Mage, 6 for Priest, 7 for Druid, 8 for Shaman/Pally (If you roll a number that in Not applicable to your race/faction, roll again.)
Professions – 1 for Enchanter, 2 for Skinner, 3 for Herbalist, 4 for alchemist, 5 for leatherworker, 6 for blacksmith, 7 for miner, 8 for Tailor, 9 for Engineering
Secondary Professions will be free to acquire.

Contestants will add to a contest thread in the Guild Events Tab to update their levels as they go. Bazzie will start the thread and then we post our character name and level as we see fit. If you die, post it in the thread. If you start again, which is highly encouraged, you can begin posting with your new character name and level updates. People may also @ someone and request their level to be posted if it has not been updated in the last 48 hours.
Please submit any questions, qualms, or concerns with the guidelines outlined above to the undersigned.

Bazzie Bazman
High Warlord and Crit Master General
